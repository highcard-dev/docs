# Druid Beta

Druid is currently in early beta.

During the beta phase, you receive weekly mana drops to test our game server hosting. The goal of the early beta is to test and improve the technology we have developed.

To participate in the beta, all you have to do is register for the [Druid app](https://app.druid.gg/signup).

You will receive an email as soon as your account is activated.

## Beta Mana System

- **Weekly Mana Drops:** Unlock up to 180 mana per week
- **Free Access:** Beta participants receive 60 mana/week automatically
- **Bonus Perks:** Verify your email (+60) and link your account (+60) for maximum drops
- See the [Mana Drops](/general/mana) page for details
